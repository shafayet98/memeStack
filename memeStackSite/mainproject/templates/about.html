About memeStack

Created by Shafayet Ul Islam

memeStack is a social platform that connects and helps meme lover to explore and share their contents globally. we use machine learning mostly computer vision technique to detect if the user is uploading meme or something else. Any content that is not considered as meme by the system is not uploadable to the server.
The pretrained model were taken from this github repository.
